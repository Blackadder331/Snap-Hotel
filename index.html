Seafront Escape

Duntrune Castle is a stunning 12th century castle that overlooks the scenic Loch Crinan in Argyll, Scotland. It has been home to the Malcolm family for over two centuries.

Ghost of Scotland

The ghost of Duntrune Castle is one of the most famous and mysterious stories in Scotland. Captured and killed by the Campbells, he played his pipes with his severed hands before he died, and his music can still be heard in the tower where he was tortured. His pipes are displayed in the castle as a reminder of his tragic fate.
